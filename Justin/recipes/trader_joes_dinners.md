# Trader Joe's Dinner Ideas

Five easy, solid dinners built almost entirely from TJ's staples.

> **Finding products:** Search any item by name at [traderjoes.com/products](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/products/pdp/cauliflower-gnocchi-061422). Note that inventory varies by location and some items are seasonal — if something's not on the shelf, ask a crew member.
>
> **Quick search:** `https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=ITEM+NAME`

## 1. Cauliflower Gnocchi with Italian Sausage and Arrabbiata

**~30 min | Serves 2–3**

### Ingredients
- 1 bag [TJ's Cauliflower Gnocchi](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=cauliflower+gnocchi)
- 1 package [TJ's Sweet Italian Chicken Sausage](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=sweet+italian+chicken+sausage), sliced into rounds
- 1 jar [TJ's Arrabbiata Sauce](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=arrabbiata+sauce)
- 1 bag [TJ's Shaved Parmesan](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=shaved+parmesan)
- Fresh basil (optional)

### Instructions
1. Cook gnocchi per bag instructions — pan-fry in olive oil over medium-high for best texture (skip the microwave).
2. In the same pan, brown sausage slices until cooked through, about 5–6 min.
3. Add arrabbiata sauce and let simmer 3–4 min.
4. Toss in gnocchi, stir to coat.
5. Serve topped with shaved parmesan and basil.

## 2. Mandarin Orange Chicken Rice Bowl

**~25 min | Serves 2–3**

### Ingredients
- 1 bag [TJ's Mandarin Orange Chicken](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=mandarin+orange+chicken)
- 1 bag [TJ's Frozen Jasmine Rice](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=frozen+jasmine+rice)
- 1 bag [TJ's Frozen Stir Fry Vegetables](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=stir+fry+vegetables)
- [TJ's Soy Sauce or Coconut Aminos](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=coconut+aminos)
- Sesame seeds, green onions to garnish

### Instructions
1. Cook orange chicken per bag instructions (oven or air fryer — air fryer at 400°F for 12 min is best).
2. Microwave jasmine rice per instructions.
3. Stir fry vegetables in a hot pan with a splash of soy sauce, 5–7 min.
4. Build bowls: rice → veggies → chicken → sauce from the orange chicken packet.
5. Top with sesame seeds and sliced green onion.

## 3. Shakshuka with Feta and Crusty Bread

**~25 min | Serves 2–3**

### Ingredients
- 1 jar [TJ's Arrabbiata or Tomato Basil Marinara](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=arrabbiata+sauce)
- 1 can [TJ's Diced Tomatoes with Green Chiles](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=diced+tomatoes+green+chiles)
- 1 container [TJ's Crumbled Feta](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=crumbled+feta)
- 4–5 eggs
- 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp smoked paprika
- [TJ's French Brioche](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=brioche) or sourdough loaf for dipping

### Instructions
1. Heat a large skillet over medium. Add a splash of olive oil.
2. Pour in marinara and diced tomatoes. Add cumin and paprika, stir.
3. Simmer sauce 5 min, then make small wells with a spoon.
4. Crack eggs into the wells. Cover the pan and cook 6–8 min until whites are set but yolks are still runny.
5. Scatter feta over the top.
6. Serve straight from the pan with thick slices of toasted bread.

## 4. Salmon with Lemon Caper Sauce and Trader Joe's Harvest Grains

**~30 min | Serves 2**

### Ingredients
- 2 [TJ's Atlantic Salmon Portions](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=atlantic+salmon) (fresh or thawed)
- 1 bag [TJ's Harvest Grains Blend](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=harvest+grains+blend)
- 2 tbsp [TJ's Capers](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=capers)
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 lemon (zest + juice)
- Garlic powder, salt, pepper

### Instructions
1. Cook harvest grains per bag instructions (~15 min on stovetop).
2. Season salmon with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Sear salmon in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high, skin-side down, 3–4 min. Flip and finish in oven at 400°F for 6–8 min.
4. In the same pan after removing salmon, melt butter over medium. Add capers, lemon juice, and zest. Swirl 1–2 min.
5. Plate grains, top with salmon, spoon caper butter sauce over everything.

## 5. TJ's Chicken Tikka Masala with Garlic Naan

**~20 min | Serves 2–3**

### Ingredients
- 1–2 packages [TJ's Chicken Tikka Masala](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=chicken+tikka+masala) (frozen entrée)
- 1 package [TJ's Garlic Naan](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=garlic+naan)
- 1 bag [TJ's Frozen Basmati Rice](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=basmati+rice)
- 1 container [TJ's Plain Whole Milk Greek Yogurt](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/search#?q=greek+yogurt) (for topping)
- Fresh cilantro (optional)

### Instructions
1. Cook tikka masala per package instructions (stovetop recommended over microwave for texture).
2. Microwave basmati rice.
3. Toast naan directly on a gas burner or in a dry skillet, 1 min per side.
4. Plate rice, ladle tikka masala over it.
5. Add a dollop of yogurt and fresh cilantro. Serve naan on the side.
