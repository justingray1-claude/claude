So Many Ways to Play

Activities

From toddlers to adults — there's something here for everyone. Serious players and casual fun-seekers welcome.

⚾

Batting Cages

Step up to the plate and take your cuts. Available for walk-ins during regular hours or book for a private rental anytime.

Private Rentals Available
⛳

Immersive VR Golf

Teleport to world-class courses without leaving Eugene. Full immersive virtual reality golf — no clubs required.

All Skill Levels
🎯

Dart Lanes

Modern dart lanes for casual fun or competitive play. Great for groups — grab a drink and throw some darts.

🕹️

Arcade

7 pinball machines, Cruisin' Exotica (free play!), claw machine, photo booth, 20+ classic games, Pop-a-Shot, Bubble Hockey, Minecraft multiplayer, and Skeeball. Bring quarters or use the change machine.

All Ages
🍺

Beer Hall

One of Eugene's finest beer halls with 30+ options on draft — beer, cider, seltzer, kombucha, and soda. Plenty of non-alcoholic options too.

🍸

Cocktail Lounge (21+)

A kid-free zone with craft cocktails, experienced bartenders, two dart boards, and TVs. Seasonal signature cocktail menu that rotates every season.

21+ Only

Always Something Fresh

Food Trucks

5 locally owned and operated food trucks on our south-facing patio. Hours vary but we always have food at PLAY.

Bounty
Charcuterie & More

Thoughtfully designed charcuterie & cheese boards, inspired sandwiches, and more. Perfect for sharing between games.

🍔 Wednesdays: Burgers & Brews — draft beverages on happy hour with any burger purchase.

Closed Mon lunch (opens 5pm). Open Tue–Sun.

Dos Agaves
Mexican

Delicious Mexican food made from scratch by locally owned 30-year Eugene residents, bringing their cultural and traditional cooking experience to share.

🌮 Taco Tuesday: rotating specials on freshly made tortillas + margarita specials in the Cocktail Lounge.

Closed Sundays.

A Little Pizza Oregon
Detroit Style Pizza

An Oregonian take on Detroit Style Pizza. Dough made with special yeast and organic flour, bulk fermented 48 hours for best flavor. Baked until the cheese around the edges is perfectly caramelized.

Closed Sunday–Tuesday.

Kazuji San
Japanese

Japanese style rice bowls from Yumi Sato — a professional in the kitchen whose experience is showcased in the quality of every bowl.

Closed Tuesdays all day. Closed lunch Wednesdays.

Paper Plate BBQ
BBQ

Moist brisket, crispy pork belly burnt ends, cheesy taters gratinadas, cheddar grits, pickled veg — BBQ that eats like a fat tray of charcuterie.

Closed Mondays. Tue–Wed 11:30am–2pm. Thu–Sun 11:30am–8pm (or sold out).

Fill Your Cup

Drinks

Whether you're looking for a cold craft beer, a seasonal cocktail, or a great non-alcoholic option — we've got you covered.

🍺

Beer Hall

One of Eugene's finest taprooms with 30+ options on draft — a diverse selection for every taste.

  • 20+ craft beers, IPAs, sours, imports
  • Ciders, seltzers, and kombucha
  • 20+ non-alcoholic options
  • 20+ craft sodas & botanically infused drinks
  • Iced teas and mocktails
🍸

Cocktail Lounge (21+)

Craft cocktails, experienced bartenders, and a rotating seasonal menu. Two dart boards and TVs — a space designed for adults.

  • Seasonal signature cocktails
  • $9 Margarita Specials on Taco Tuesdays
  • Craft beer + cocktail pairing
  • Kid-free environment
🎵

Weekly Drink Events

Regular events in the lounge keep things interesting all week long.

  • Thursdays 6pm — PLAY: Records (all-vinyl DJ sets)
  • Fridays biweekly 8pm — Soul Sessions (electronic)
  • Last Saturday monthly 8pm — Reggae Riddims
  • Wednesdays — Burgers & Brews happy hour
