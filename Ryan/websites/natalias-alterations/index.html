Formal Wear

Everyday Alterations

Specialty Projects

Leather patches, curtain shortening, and unique alteration requests — if it's fabric, Natalia can work with it.

🌸

Seasonal Scheduling Notice

From March through September, the shop focuses almost exclusively on wedding and formal wear — and books at least one week out. For everyday alterations, October through February is the best time to visit. All visits are by appointment only.

About Natalia

Craftsmanship at the Heart of Eugene

Natalia has built a loyal following in Eugene through word-of-mouth, repeat customers, and a reputation for getting it right. Located inside the historic 5th Street Public Market, her shop is a trusted destination for anyone who needs their clothes to fit the way they should.

Whether you're preparing for a wedding, a job interview, or just want your favorite jeans to fit perfectly, Natalia brings the same care and precision to every project — big or small.
